MES COLLEGE of Engineering

kuttipuram,malappuram

MES College of Engineering was established in year 1994 and is located in Malappuram, Kerala. It offers courses at UG and PG level with highly experienced faculty in Full Time mode. Approved by the CoA and AICTE, the institute offers multiple courses such as B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Arch, M.E./M.Tech, M.Arch and MCA courses. Furthermore, it offers several specialisations in the fields of Architecture & Planning, IT & Software, Engineering and Business & Management Studies. MES College of Engineering offers these courses within the range of INR 1,50,000-4,02,000 with the intent to provide education at highly affordable fees. The institute offers 794 seats, encouraging more candidates to gain knowledge and enhance their skills.
